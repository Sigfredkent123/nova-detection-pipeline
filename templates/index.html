What is NoVA-SCAN

NoVA-Scan is an AI-powered web application that automatically detects key areas from your image — the Eyes, Palm, and Nail Beds — using advanced object detection technology (YOLO).

Eyes

Let NoVA-Scan detect your eye region automatically for a clear and accurate image.

Palm

Your palm area is identified instantly, helping the AI prepare it for detailed analysis.

Nail Beds

NoVA-Scan pinpoints your nail area so the system can focus on the right details.

How It Works

1

Capture or Upload

Take a photo or upload an image showing your eyes, palm, or nails using your device’s camera.

2

AI Detection

NoVA-Scan uses advanced YOLO object detection to automatically locate and outline key regions.

3

Review Results

View the detected areas and confirm accuracy before saving the output.
